My blog post

February 12, 2021 by Andrew

This is my opinion on recent events, the news is included, but this is the opinion and thoughts of the chosen contributor.

Yeah, i'm just gonna say it; Nintendo, what were you thinking with the motion control!?

This new update has made the joycon drift outrageously with the most simple controls, in game and in the console menu. The hardware seems to not be taking the update well, as the controllers have be seen to become hotter and have less charge within an hour

Trying to connect the dots, don't know what to tell my fellow gamers. Nintendo was trying to fix regular software, and has refused to revert to the previous version in the last 3 updates, which has been over this last month

Major Point

Suiting up for my crowning battle, if you only knew what the future holds, I will gladly complain on a national stage in order to get this fixed. We all spent over 900000 hours over the last week, and the company has not addressed us.

Side Point

You give a hundred reasons why to get mad, and you say you're really gonna try to fix the console. Straight lies made worse by the simple statement put out by VP of the company below:

We are sorry for the mistake. We will address it as soon as we can. Thank you!

No real problem mentioned, no solution proposed, no promise of compensation, this is just not acceptable.

Final Point

You got the finest communtiy in the world and worldwide acceptance, please address this better. I could list 3 things that the company could do to alleviate this debacle and gain some respect back. The list are as follows:

  • Pause all outgoing consoles, at least internationally, if not domestically.
  • Have a mandatory software reboot to the last stable version, version 3.2.2.
  • And allow users to have at least 2 complimentary joycons if theirs were damaged.

Please take this into consideration and give me back my $40.
